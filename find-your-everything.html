Find Your Everything

A short experience and abstract representation of real life dating

About The Project

FYE was developed for the Ludum Dare 45 game jam that had a duration of 3 days and whose theme was "Start with nothing". It is an experimental game where we try to create a puzzling analogy to what finding the love of your life might be like in real life.

The final result wasn't as polished as we intended to, due to lack of time, but we got plenty of good feedback.

We didn't touch the game after the jam finished, but we will certainly give it some final touches in our spare time.

My Role In The Project

  • Game Design
