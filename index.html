Who am I?

Long story short, I'm a person passionate about game development and the entertainment industry.

I am a 20 year-old man from Lisbon, Portugal who has been passionate about the entertainment industry for as long as I can remember. So I started my graduation 3 years ago and now I'm only an internship away from having a Graduate Degree in Game Development.

In college, I formed a group called Redoak Studios with 3 colleagues, which was responsible for the development of many games. We accomplished great results, earning many respected awards from my University and other organizations which you can check in my Curriculum Vitae.

Thanks to many past projects, I have developed great skills working as a team and individually, not only for bigger projects but also smaller ones such as game jams - having participated on several throughout my journey as a game developer.

Aside from the game dev world, I am also an energetic individual who practices combat sports. I'm a Kickboxing and Muay Thai athlete and I've even won a Kickboxing National Champion title.

What are my main qualifications?

Programming

I specialize in C# and object-oriented programming but I have worked with many other languages such as C, JavaScript and HTML both in game development and other applications aswell.

Game Design

One of my most consistant roles in game development has been game design, which I had in all of my previous projects. Other extensions of this area such as World and Level Design and have also been a part of my work.

2D Art & Graphic Design

UI design, promo-art, image manipulation and graphic design are all things I've worked on and am familiarized with. I specialize in Photoshop and have basic knowledge in vector-based programs such as Illustrator.
