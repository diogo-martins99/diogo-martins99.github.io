Who am I?

Long story short, I'm a man passionate about game development and the entertainment industry.

I am a 20 year-old man from Lisbon, Portugal who has been passionate about the entertainment industry for as long as I can remember. With only an internship left to complete, I am close to having a Graduate Degree in Game Development.

I accomplished great results throughout my graduation, having earned many respected prizes from my University ULHT and some other entities which you can read in more detail in my Curriculum Vitae (editar isto depois com os prémios corretos e menções).

What are my main qualifications?

Programming

I specialize in C# and object-oriented programming but I have worked with many other languages such as C, JavaScript and HTML both in game development and other applications aswell.

Game Design

One of my most consistant roles in game development has been game design, which I had in all of my previous projects. Other extensions of this area such as World and Level Design and have also been a part of my work.

2D Art & Graphic Design

UI design, promo-art, image manipulation and graphic design are all things I've worked on and am familiarized with. I specialize in Photoshop and have basic knowledge in vector-based programs such as Illustrator.
