Synopsis

ATMU is a 2D side-scroller game, where an abandoned robot tries to find his way home by overcoming obstacles and solving puzzles on a deserted planet.

  • Download Link

About The Project

ATMU was developed in about 4 months alongside Illuminants. It was a 1st year project and the one that most developed my programming skills in that year. The game design and level design were also incredibly challenging specially given the experience my team and I had, but I'm proud to say that it worked out great and I gained a great amount of experience from it.

The game as been showcased in many events and has won an Honorable Mention award given by my university.
