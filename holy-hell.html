Synopsis

Holy Hell is an action-adventure game set in Heaven, where you are searching for God so you can avenge your death. Fight angels, mythical beings, the Seven Virtues and God himself as you venture through all corners of Heaven.

About The Project

Holy Hell was a 6 month project that I developed in Unity, along with 3 colleagues of mine, for my last graduation year. Aside from improving skills that I already had (e.g. programming), it helped me develop skills in project management and marketing aswell.

For now, it will remain a vertical slide.

My Role In The Project

  • Programming

    I was Holy Hell's lead programmer, focusing mostly on gameplay.

  • Game Design

    I've worked on both game design and level design side-by-side with my colleagues.

  • UI Design

    With the use of Photoshop, I created Holy Hell's UI components, later tweaking and adding visual effects in Unity.

  • 2D Art

    I was also responsible for creating promo-art and official documents for the game such as the Artbook or the Spec Sheet.

  • Marketing

    Besides the creation of posters, banners and other promotional art, I was the project's marketing manager in charge of many social networks (Instagram, Twitter, IndieDB, etc) and all communications with the press.
