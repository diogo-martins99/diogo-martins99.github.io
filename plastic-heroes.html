Synopsis

A play to earn runner where you play as a bird, unique in style and gameplay, flapping your wings to dodge all sorts of hazards.

Videos

About The Project

CryptoBird is a client project developed for Exeedme and it is their first Play2Earn blockchain game. It combines in-game characters as NFTs with a rewards pool to incentivize a vibrant gaming community and encompass the true play to earn mechanics.

My Role In The Project

  • Fast Prototyping

    I was responsible for the fast prototyping of the initial concept behind this game.

  • Gameplay Programming

    I implemented gameplay systems, mechanics and other specific tools that helped the development of the project.

  • Game Design

    I took part in the game design process and some balancing.
