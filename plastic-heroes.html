Synopsis

You're a toy in a small but dangerous world where other evil toys will try to kill you! Join your friends and see how far you can get!

About The Project

Plastic Heroes is an in-house project that we're developing at Fun Punch Games, aimed to launch for mobile and Nintendo Switch.
This is a very ambitious project but also one of our favorites. It is the first time we're developing a multiplayer FPS game and we believe this will be a major successful IP to add to our collection.

My Role In The Project

  • Gameplay Programming

    I implemented gameplay systems, mechanics and specific tools that helped the development of the project.

  • Game Design

    I took part in the game design process and some balancing.
