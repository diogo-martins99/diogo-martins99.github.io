Bernardino

The epic physics-based adventure of an elastic man

About The Project

Bernardino was a Unity project developed in 4 months my group and I for the last semester of the second year. Since we we're working on two big projects for that semester - Bernardino and Heróis do Mar - we decided to do a proper division of tasks. So we all worked on the game design of both projects, but split into 2 groups for the rest of their development, still side-by-side giving feedback and sharing thoughts.

Bernardino was an incredibly challenging project, as I had to implement a lot of physics behaviours. I also took my first steps in procedural animation for this project and improved my AI skills by tackling the bosses.

For now, the project will remain a vertical slice.

My Role In The Project

Synopsis

Bernardino is a third-person physics-based adventure game full of challenging obstacles and giant monsters. Using your elastic powers, venture through its dangerous world to save your village’s ancient wizard, you are the chosen one after all!
